VISITING BAR HARBOR

Dock at Bar Harbor

Bar Harbor

Bar Harbor is a beautiful town and one of the most popular tourist destinations in the United States. It has unparalleled historic charm, a beautiful coastline and many restaurants and shops It is the perfect town to spend the rest of the day after adventures at Acadia National Park.

The best way to start your visit to Bar Harbor is to take a slow stroll along Shore Path. It is a lovely coastal trail going back to 1880 which starts next to Agamond Park at the Town's Ells Pier. After you pass the historic Bar Harbor Inn, the path wraps around a point and then continues south along the eastern shore of the island. You will pass many magnificent palaces called cottages.

When you come back to the start of Shore Path, just sit awhile on one of the many benches. The view over the water is simply breathtaking.

There are many restaurants and shops to explore in Bar Harbor. Take a walk down Main Street, pop in some stores and try out some restaurants.
